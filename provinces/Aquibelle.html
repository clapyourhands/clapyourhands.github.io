Aquibelle

Fields of Plenty

Aquibelle is a fertile farming community blessed with abundance thanks to the rich volcanic soil that spills from Bael's Crater. Ailell Fields is the breadbasket of western Astalon. Though volcanic eruptions have devastated the province in decades past, it has been a long while since the last eruption. The Chateau-Imperiale protects Aquibelle from Sakhin raiders from the northeast. Recently constructed with the aid of the city of Wyndam, the Ailell Canal undercuts Falinbrook to the southwest by granting faster access to and from the heartland--with tolls going to Aquibelle, of course. This has been a source of emnity between Falinbrook and Aquibelle.







Ailell Fields

The Ailell Fields are fertilized by ash from Bael's Crater. The land here is owned by a number of aristocratic families and provides much of the food that feeds western Astalon.







Bael's Crater

Though this volcano is active, it rarely spews lava. Instead, it constantly releases plumes of ash that blanket the surrounding areas, giving both the Smoky River and the Ailell Fields their signature hues.







Cheateau-Imperiale

A grand series of guard towers connected by walls that form an imposing border between Aquibelle and the Great Expanse. Manned year-round by trained watchmen, the towers have a sophisticated relay system to notify one another of incursions.
