Dornsmere

The Dusky Isles

Dornsmere, the capital city, is a nondescript place, famous more for its exotic orc-influenced culture than any of the goods it produces. The Ballyhork Settlements on the Ballyhork Islands, instead, produce the bulk of the province's wealth. Transporting stone from the productive Capstone Quarry as well as foreign goods imported into Falinbrook, the Dornsmere government has a well-oiled agreement with the Biggus orc tribe to protect their trade in exchange for a cut of the profits. The Slitwood Forest in the northern part of the province is dense and ominous, named so because light only barely passes through tiny slits in the dense canopy.







Ballyhork Islands

Actually soft marshland, the Ballyhork Islands were formerly hills that have become partially inundated with water. The orc tribes that dwell here are skilled in sailing on shallow banks and maneuvering amphibiously between land and sea.







Capstone Quarry

Though part of Aquibelle Province, Capstone Quarry is economically very closely linked to Dornsmere. The dwarvish enclave of Capstone City is located within these bountiful mines.







Dusky Sea

Sooty from the copious ash blown out of Bael's Crater, the water of the Dusky Sea is permanently murky. Somewhere to the distant northwest is the continent of Kolkun.
