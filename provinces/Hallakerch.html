Hallakerch

Radiant Bridge

Hallakerch Province centers around the two great cities of Kerch, located on the north bank of the Great Astalon River, and Halla, located on the south bank. A few centuries ago, the cities merged with the building of a stone bridge, becoming the trading port of Hallakerch. The city of Verglade, located on the high cliffs of southeastern Astalon, specializes in diving for pearls and climbing the Spires of Lian for rare seabird eggs and feathers. Because the Spires and steep cliffside render the waters around Verglade unnavigable, though, Hallakerch is the most well-suited location for trade between the Misty and Radiant Seas. This commerce granted the city enough influence to move the capital closer to themselves, from the distant Wickhamford to nearby Ruelan. Today, Hallakerch thrives by providing the populous and wealthy inhabitants of Ruelan luxury goods from afar (as well as the less-wealthy inhabitants cheaper wares). Robe's Nest is located near Templar Mount, and is where less dedicated monks may indulge in city life with one foot still in the monastery. Also in this province is Harrow's Pass, a narrow gap between the Misty Sea and the southern end of Sundering Gorge that connects into the Great Expanse. To protect against encroaching Sakhin raiders, the kingdom built Fort Bocca at this chokepoint and always keeps it well-armed with royal soldiers.







Great Astalon River

The Great Astalon River connects the Glass Fjords in the north to the Radiant Sea in the east. It provides almos tall of the fresh water used by the enormous cities of Ruelan and Hallakerch.







Hallakerch Bridge

A feat of both engineering as well as political manuevering, Hallakerch Bridge spans either end of the Great Astalon River and plays a large role in the city's commercial dominance.







Radiant Sea

The waters of the Radiant Sea get their name from how they seem to glow in the rising sun. The continent of Cochinia lies far over the eastern horizon.
