Kindleton

The Last Bastion

The furthest settlement of western Astaloni civilization until the Great Expanse, Kindleton is protected by the Kindlebrush Mountains to the east and Myrdin Lake to the west, keeping Sakhin raiders at bay. Still, traders coming in and out of the city will find the journey treacherous; Furia Outpost on the eastern edge of the Dunborre Desert was built to give travelers a place to safely rest during the journey. Fishtail Island is populous for its size, providing Kindleton proper with an abundance of freshwater catches. The illustrious Haven Bridge is the pride of the province; it is said that in the event of a Sakhin siege, the bridge can even be raised to keep the horse riders away from Fishtail Island. Though technically not part of western Astalon geographically, the province nevertheless has close political, cultural, and economic ties to the coastal regions, and thus is traditionally grouped alongside them.







Myrdin Lake

A beautifully pure lake at one end of the Calabash River. It provides both food and shelter for the people of Fishtail Island, who are wary of outsiders and rarely venture over Haven Bridge for anything other than trade.







Dunborre Desert

The unforgiving Dunborre Desert separates Kindleton from western Astalon. Only experienced travelers can navigate its boiling sands safely, and many have perished in failure. Lake Merrin is the only source of potable water between Kindleton and Wickhamford.







Furia Outpost

Furia Outpost defends western Astalon from Sakhin incursions. With the Smokestone and Kindlebrush Mountains as chokepoints, horse raiders that wish to cross the desert first have to contend with the well-trained force stationed at this edge of civilization.
