Ruelan

The Great City

Ruelan Province is the kingdom capital; it is the largest city in area, and if one includes the surrounding suburbs, it is the largest city by population as well. The capital was moved from Wickhamford as part of a political agreement with the city of Hallakerch to the south. Also in this province is Templar Mount, a religious fortress isolated from the rest of society. It sits by the Sundering Gorge near its widest point, Devil's Maw, allowing its members to gaze into the black depths and contemplate their faith. The town of Tithen subsists more or less entirely based on ferrying holiday-goers to King's Island, the royal playground. Though closed when the royal family is staying there, the island is otherwise open year-round to visitors who wish to camp, fish, or just relax in the wilderness.







The Capital

The crowded city of Ruelan is truly a sight to behold. Slums and palaces alike dot the capital in close quarters; nowhere in Astalon are people and buildings more densely packed together than here.







Templar Mount

Templar Mount serves as a monastery for faithful monks. Some say it was built as a counterpart to Wickhamford's Saint Quarters in the east of the continent.







King's Island

Ruelan's well-off can spend their leisure time on the secluded King's Island. A rare breath of fresh air and open space for city-dwellers, the island cost King Sarchen a small fortune to develop centuries ago.
