Ur-Bakhar

Roving Plains

An Astaloni province only in name, Ur-Bakhar is dominated by Sakhin tribes. The region has no true cities to speak of, as its inhabitants are pastoral horse riders. However, four small settlements dot the corners of the province: Ersakhin, Orsakhin, Lohsakhin, and Helsakhin. Named after the sun, sky, moon, and stars in the Sakhi language respectively, they serve as religious sites where tribe members may gather. Periodically, the tribes will also meet in the central Ur-Bakhar Ordun, where group disputes are settled and marriages are sanctified.







The Great Expanse

An impossibly vast plain that makes up the center of Astalon. The continent's wild, untamed heart stretches as far as the eye can see, serving almost as much of a barrier as the chasm to its north.







Four Orduns

The four Orduns are landmarks and meeting points for the Sakhin. They believe that one's temperament and fortune depend on which Ordun they were born near. Troubled Sakhin families can always find solace in an Ordun.







Loh-E Ordun

Loh-E Ordun is the largest, central Ordun. It is also known as Ur-Bakhar, which Dolomir the Great named the so-called province after. All important Sakhin religious and political ceremonies take place here.
